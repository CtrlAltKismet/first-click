Need a Little Help?

This page explains how to use ClickStart and answers common questions. If you're new to computers or digital skills, don't worry -- everything on this site is designed to be simple and beginner friendly.

Understanding ClickStart

ClickStart is organised into simple sections to help you learn digital skills step-by-step.

Learn

The Learn page contains beginner friendly lessons explaining important computer skills and online tools.

Practice

The Practice page includes small activities that help you try what you've learned and build confidence.

Quiz

Test your knowledge with a short quiz to see how much you remember from the lessons.

Common Questions

Here are answers to questions beginners often have when using the ClickStart.

Do I need experience?

No. ClickStart is designed for complete beginners. Everything is explained step-by-step.

Can I use this on my phone?

Yes. The website works on phones, tablets and ocmputers using responsive design.

Can I make mistakes?

Yes! Making mistakes is part of learning. Take your time and try again. ClickStart is designed for beginners who may be using a computer for the first time.

Do I need an account?

No account is required. All lessons and activities are free to access.

Can I go at my own pace?

Yes. You can read lessons and complete activities whenever you like.

What if I don't understand something?

Take your time and revisit the lesson again. Learning digital skills takes practice. Alternatively, you can type in words you don't understand below and it will tell you the definition!
